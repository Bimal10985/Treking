About Us

Namaste! Nepal, A small, independent country never invaded, oldest country of South Asia, elevation ranging from 68m to 8848m above the sea level will be your perfect travel destination. Nepal is home for 8 out of 10 highest mountains of world. With Triangular national flag, Nepal is the only country in world to have non rectangular flag. Here are 125 ethnic group/caste, 123 languages are spoken but no single drop of blood shed on the name of religion clash, might be because of home of Living Goddess (Kumari) and Birthplace of Gautam Buddha (Founder of Buddhism).
Nepal can be your next travel destination; here at Black Stork Trek and Expedition we offer Trekkings, Expeditions services. This company was founded by travel enthusiast, being trekking guide for more than 5 years to provide mesmerizing services to travelers. Still most of the rural parts of Nepal are without proper school for children, basic facilities, taking few days to reach the main city area. To promote the rural areas, basic education, establishing libraries, providing health care, empowering the locals to earn livelihood is an objective of Black Stork Trek and Expedition. The small profit earned from numerous clients can really help us to achieve this objective. Thank you for reaching out, contact us to book trek/know more.
